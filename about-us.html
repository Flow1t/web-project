Background

poshana community

This community is formed by companies who have been in the line of Design, Printing, and Packaging industry for many years, as a means of educating the market. We specialize in elevating Small Medium Enterprises beyond their current confined local market through thoughtfully designed, well printed labels, and packaging to comply with international standards.

For the past few years, each member has continuously educated the public regarding the benefits of high quality printing in their own ways. In 2023, we joined forces and collaborated as one community in order to assist more SMEs in getting ready for global market penetration.
