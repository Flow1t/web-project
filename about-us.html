Background

poshana community

This community is formed by companies who have been in the line of Design, Printing and Packaging industry for many years, as a means of educating the market, especially the Small Medium Enterprises, on how to elevate their brands beyond their current confined local market through thoughtfully designed and well printed labels and packaging to comply with the international standard.

For the past few years each members has continuously educated the public regarding the benefit of high quality printing in their own ways, but in 2023 we got together and collaborate as one community in order to assist more SMEs in getting ready for global market penetration.

Event Activities and Objectives

main event

Our main events comprised of online and offline open classroom or pre-selected participants training, workshop, brand consultation, business coaching, re-branding packagings and working with the local communities and government all over Indonesia for the post events follow-ups.

objectives

The purpose of our events is to support and nurture as many SMEs as we possibly can to understand the importance of good design and good quality printing and packaging to extend the lifespan of their products, the brand awareness in the market and increase the sales value against the fast moving trends.

Pre-Events Preparations

komunitas lokal dan atau pemerintah

To increase the effectiveness of the events, we usually need all this information regarding the SMEs before we decide on when, where, and how we are going to conduct our events:

Total number of SMEs in the area
Product category, variant and current packaging sizes
Production capacity, packaging process and market share
Current sales value of the products
Intention and target of the local SMEs in regards of elevating their products packaging
